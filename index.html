Three-Pillar Workflow: Core Focus Planning

Client Profile: Business owners with 5+ year or uncertain exit timeline, wealth building focus

Phase 1: Core Assessment & Wealth Building Analysis (Weeks 1-3)

Duration: 3 weeks | Focus: Long-term wealth optimization

Week 1 Activities

Lead Advisor Business Growth Strategy Assessment
• Business growth plans and capital needs
• Reinvestment vs. distribution preferences
• Management succession and development
• Market expansion and diversification plans
Technical Advisor Wealth Building Analysis
• Current asset allocation and diversification
• Business vs. investment asset balance
• Liquidity needs and cash flow planning
• Risk tolerance and investment objectives
Future Exit Option Preservation
• Maintaining flexibility for future exit planning
• Annual exit interest assessment protocol

Week 2 Activities

Technical Advisor Current Tax Optimization
• Business growth phase tax strategies
• Equipment and expansion tax benefits
• Retirement plan optimization
• Multi-year tax planning opportunities
Lead Advisor Current Estate Planning Review
• Existing trust and entity structures
• Insurance coverage adequacy
• Beneficiary and succession planning
• Asset protection considerations
Both Advisors Charitable Giving Assessment
• Current giving patterns and motivations
• Tax benefit optimization opportunities
• Long-term philanthropic objectives

Week 3 Activities

Joint Session Three-Pillar Integration Analysis
• Tax optimization with business growth
• Charitable giving coordination with wealth building
• Generational transfer while maintaining control
• Wealth diversification and risk management
Technical Advisor Advanced Strategy Identification
• Minority interest gifting opportunities
• Grantor trust strategies for growth assets
• Charitable remainder trust for diversification
• Family limited partnership structuring

Phase 2: Core Strategy Development (Weeks 4-6)

Duration: 3 weeks | Focus: Wealth building optimization
Pillar 1: Tax Minimization (Growth Focus)
• Business reinvestment tax strategies
• Equipment and expansion tax benefits
• Income timing and smoothing strategies
• Section 199A deduction optimization
• State tax planning for business operations
Pillar 2: Charitable Giving Optimization
• Donor-advised fund establishment
• Private foundation feasibility analysis
• Appreciated business interest gifts
• Charitable remainder trust for diversification
• Income tax deduction timing optimization
Pillar 3: Generational Transfer (Control Retention)
• Minority interest annual exclusion gifts
• Grantor retained annuity trust (GRAT) strategies
• Sales to defective grantor trusts
• Family limited partnership creation
• Dynasty trust planning for growth assets
Future Exit Planning Preparation
• Structures that accommodate future exit planning
• Valuation discount strategies
• Management and succession development

Phase 3: Client Education & Presentation (Weeks 7-8)

Duration: 2 weeks | Focus: Long-term strategy presentation
Presentation Structure (75 minutes)
• Current state and wealth building assessment (15 min)
• Three-pillar core strategy presentation (45 min)
• Implementation timeline and flexibility (15 min)
Annual Exit Planning Review Protocol
• Annual assessment of exit interest and timeline
• Framework transition to four-pillar if applicable
• Strategy modification for changing objectives

Phase 4: Core Implementation (Weeks 9-14)

Duration: 6 weeks | Focus: Flexible long-term structures
Tax Strategy Implementation
• Business entity optimization
• Retirement plan establishment/optimization
• Equipment purchase and timing strategies
• Multi-year tax planning setup
Charitable Structure Implementation
• Donor-advised fund or foundation setup
• Charitable giving policy development
• Tax-efficient giving structure implementation
Generational Transfer Implementation
• Family limited partnership formation
• Trust structure establishment
• Initial minority interest gifts
• Annual gifting program setup
Monitoring and Review Setup
• Quarterly review protocol establishment
• Annual exit assessment scheduling
• Performance tracking system setup

Ongoing Management: Annual Framework Assessment

Annual Review Components
• Business growth and exit timeline reassessment
• Framework transition evaluation (three to four-pillar)
• Strategy effectiveness and optimization review
• Tax law changes and planning updates
• Family situation and objective changes
